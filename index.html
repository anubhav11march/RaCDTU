Welcome

The Rotaract Club of Delhi Technological University Regency brings together like-minded individuals to serve the community and develop themselves as people.

Established in 2012, the Rotaract Club of DTU Regency is an active society on the campus, organizing frequent drives, recurring Melas, and online campaigns.

  • Find out what we do

Diwali Mela

Each Diwali, RaCDTU conducts a Diwali Mela. Rotaractors organize several engaging games for the Mela. With a multitude of short fun activities, the Mela helps raise funds for a generous cause.

Lantern lighting follows the Diwali Mela. Environmental-friendly lanterns are lit by the students and released into the sky.

Blood Donation Camps

A single blood donation can save as many as three lives. RaCDTU organizes frequent blood donation camps in tha DTU campus to promote donation and collect blood for the welfare of people.


Online Campaigns

Frequent campaigns against the likes of depression, body-shaming, peer-pressure, among other evils, are conducted by RaCDTU on our online platforms. RaCDTU provides a safe space to share experiences.

Rotaract Global Model United Nations (Special Edition) 2018

RaCDTU was selected to host the 2018 special edition of Rotaract Global Model United Nations. The 3-day international event involved hundreds of delegates. The reports generated by the various committees simulated were sent to the UN Headquarters for introspection. RGMUN (2018 SE) involved several prominent speakers.
